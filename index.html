Suneel Babu Chatla, Ph.D.

Assistant Professor, Department of Mathematical Sciences, The University of Texas at El Paso

I am a statistician working on high-dimensional methodology, nonparametric and semiparametric models, functional data analysis, statistical computing, and deep learning.

My research develops theory and computation for modern data problems, especially when data are complex, dependent, function-valued, or high-dimensional. Recent work includes longitudinal quantile regression, robust variable selection, inverse regression for spatially distributed functional data, and deep learning methods for scalar-on-function models.

Before joining UTEP, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Institute of Statistics, National Tsing Hua University, Taiwan. I received my Ph.D. from National Tsing Hua University in 2017.
